About the Project

This project focuses on analyzing the relationship between global raw cotton prices and the prices of textiles. The goal is to understand how fluctuations in cotton commodity prices impact textile production costs and consumer pricing. Using machine learning and statistical analysis, the study explores patterns in cotton price volatility, investigates key factors influencing these fluctuations, and develops predictive models to forecast future cotton prices. Insights from this research aim to support textile manufacturers and related industries in making informed decisions regarding production and pricing strategies.
